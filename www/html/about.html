Way Back When

Justin Bull is a computer programmer who has had a fascination with technology since childhood. His interest in digital media began at the age of 9 in 1998 with MS paint on Windows 98. Much later on, Justin discovered the joy of vector graphics with Adobe Flash. It was then his passion for programming blossomed. His most accomplished feat was learning to use Autodesk’s 3DS Max without the means of conventional education.

Community Service

AS a young boy, Justin was a member of Scouts Canada for 4 years and voluntarily offered his services in wild life preservation, charity drives, and community clean up.

School

During elementary school, Justin displayed a keen interest in Science. With months of hard work, he won 3 district awards and a certificate of achievement.

Sports

Justin has been active in competitive sports most his life including baseball, soccer, rugby, cross country running, and swimming. He joined the Mouat Hawks swim team in 2003 and after 4 years of persistence and daily practice, he won multiple awards including Top Senior, Most Improved, and Fireman as well as receiving 1st place in the BC swim championship. His strengths were the 50m Freestyle, and 200m Individual Medley which is a test of endurance utilizing all four strokes.
