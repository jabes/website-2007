About JB Design Studio

Brief History

My name is Justin Bull, and I am the founder of JB Design Studio. I was born in New Westminster. I’ve lived in Abbotsford all my life, but recently moved to Coquitlam. I’m taking New Media and Web Design at BCIT’s Burnaby campus. Basically, that is all I will be doing for the next year.

Hobbies

I enjoy working with my hands. It is a personality trait given to me by my father. That combined with curiosity usually ends badly. I’m always taking things apart to see how they work, only to find they don’t go back together as easily. That said, one of my largest passions other than art, is cars. One may tell by looking at my artwork.

I’m also a very active person. I enjoy playing many sports such as competitive swimming, soccer, and football. One of my favorite summer time activities is mountain trail riding. Many years ago I rode across the kettle valley. It was an experience that words can’t describe.

My personality best reflects my mother. I love to write, I perfect everything I do, and I worry too much about appearance. I owe a lot to my parents. I wouldn’t be where I am, or who I am without them.

My Favorite Quote
